About Us

Princess Swan Designs was created when Tonyce Cooke and Shelby Swanson became best friends freshman year of college. They met in the dorms and instantly clicked. We both enjoy crafting and business, so we thought having a home decor business of handmade items would be a great way to make a few extra bucks while going to school. Although we are still in the process of getting started we feel like we will be successful and have a profitable online business heading into our senior years at the University of Montana.
