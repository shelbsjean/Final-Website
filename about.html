About Me

I was born in Corpus Christi, Texas in Feburary of 1995. In 2002 my family had moved to the small coal-mining town of Colstrip, Montana. With little else to do, I began drawing. From doodles to paintings, I spent most of my introverted teenage years improving my hand and eye at the craft of creation

In 2013, a few months after graduation from high school, I moved to Missoula, Montana to pursue a career in art. After a few years, I settled on the idea of doing graphic design and animation professionally. I'm finishing up my last semesters at the University of Montana's media arts program.
